Productivity

Thoughts on Productivity by Jhon Acevedo on Jan 20st, 2024

  • Write Essays to Articulate Clear Thinking
  • Work on things I care about and like
  • Don't work on things I don't care about or don't like. Outsource, Avoid, or Delegate to someone who like it
  • Schedule time to think about what to work on
  • I can learn anything I want. I can get better quickly. Trust that I can do it as it has been done many times in the past
  • Surround myself with people who are smart, productive, happy, proactive, and above all, high integrity
  • Always be high integrity
  • Do the work. Work both smart and hard
  • Be around people that push and inspire me to be better
  • Prioritize getting important shit done daily, weekly, monthly...
  • Write down list of priorities. Transcribe to each new day, adding metadata. Excercise the prioritization muscle
  • Be relentless in getting the most important projects done
  • Invest time finding the thing to work on. Work at it with blistering intensity
  • Be ruthless about saying no to things
  • Do non-critical things in the quickest way possible
  • Avoid meetings & conferences
  • Sleep in a cold, dark, quiet room with a face mask
  • Don't eat hours before you sleep
  • Avoid Sugar
  • Keep junk food outside the office. I have an obsessive personality. Direct it intentionally and create environments condusive to success
  • Omega 3, Vitamin D-3 are always friendly
  • Clean workspace. My desk is reflective of the state of mind. Clear office = clear thinking
  • Avoid people who put you in a bad mood. Protect your energy
